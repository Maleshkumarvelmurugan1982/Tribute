About The Legend

Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, known professionally as Vijay, was born on June 22, 1974, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. He is one of the highest-paid and most popular actors in Tamil cinema, often referred to as "Thalapathy" (Commander) by his fans.


Son of veteran filmmaker S. A. Chandrasekhar and playback singer Shoba, Vijay started his career as a child artist and made his debut as a lead actor in 1992. Over three decades, he has established himself as a commercial force with numerous blockbusters and has a massive fan following across the globe.


Known for his dancing skills, charismatic screen presence, and social consciousness, Vijay has become more than just an actor‚Äîhe's a cultural phenomenon and a voice for social change.

Beyond acting, Vijay is one of the most prolific singers among Tamil actors. He made his singing debut with "Bombay City Sukkha Rotti" in 1994 and has sung over 35+ songs for legendary composers including A.R. Rahman, Ilaiyaraaja, Anirudh Ravichander, and Yuvan Shankar Raja.


Career Milestone: After singing 25 songs, he took a sabbatical in 2005 to focus on acting, but made a spectacular comeback with "Google Google" in Thuppakki (2012), which won Favorite Song of the Year at Vijay Awards!


International Fame: His song "Selfie Pulla" from Kaththi became a viral sensation in Romania and other European countries in 2016, earning him a Filmfare Award nomination for Best Playback Singer.

Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK)

In February 2024, Vijay made a historic announcement by launching his political party, Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK), marking his official entry into Tamil Nadu politics. This move came after years of speculation about his political aspirations.

February 2, 2024

Official announcement of Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK) party formation

October 27, 2024

First state conference held in Vikravandi, Villupuram district with massive attendance

Party Ideology

Secular social justice principles, opposition to divisive forces, emphasis on equality and rationalism

Future Plans

Planning to contest Tamil Nadu Assembly elections in 2026

Political Stand & Ideology

Vijay's political stance emphasizes:

  • Secularism: Strongly opposes communalism and divisive politics
  • Social Justice: Focus on equality and uplifting marginalized communities
  • Anti-Corruption: Commitment to transparent governance
  • Youth Empowerment: Special focus on education and employment opportunities
  • Rationalism: Inspired by Dravidian ideological principles
  • Women's Rights: Strong advocacy for gender equality and women's safety

Social Activism

Even before entering politics formally, Vijay was known for his social consciousness:

  • Regular charitable contributions through Vijay Makkal Iyakkam
  • Educational scholarships for underprivileged students
  • Disaster relief efforts during floods and COVID-19 pandemic
  • Support for farmers' welfare and addressing agricultural issues
  • Advocacy against social evils and corruption through his films

Vijay announced he would step away from cinema after completing his current commitments to focus entirely on politics, making 2026 a pivotal year for both his cinematic farewell and political debut.
